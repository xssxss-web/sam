We offer end-to-end sourcing and supply chain services from Yiwu, China. Whether you need a single product sourced or a full container consolidated from multiple suppliers, we handle it all.

---

## Product Sourcing & Supplier Matching

We find reliable factories for your target products. Our local team visits suppliers, verifies credentials, negotiates prices, and arranges samples.

- Factory audit and verification
- Price negotiation on your behalf
- Sample collection and coordination
- Supplier comparison and recommendation

---

## Quality Control & Inspection

Before your goods leave China, we inspect them. You receive photos, videos, and a detailed inspection report.

- Pre-production sample check
- In-line production inspection
- Pre-shipment final inspection (AQL standard)
- Loading supervision

---

## Mixed Wholesale Consolidation

Combine products from different suppliers into one shipment. No need to meet each factory's MOQ separately.

- Multi-supplier order consolidation
- Warehouse receipt and sorting
- Carton marking and labeling
- Mixed container loading

For Amazon sellers: we prepare your goods according to FBA requirements and ship directly to Amazon warehouses.

- FNSKU labeling
- Poly bagging and bundling
- Carton labeling (FBA box labels)
- Palletizing if required
