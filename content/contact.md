## What to Include in Your Inquiry

To give you the most accurate quotation, please include:

- **Product details** — name, description, photos, or reference links
- **Target quantity** — trial order size and estimated monthly volume
- **Target price range** — your budget per unit (if you have one)
- **Customization needs** — logo, packaging, color, material preferences
- **Destination country** — for shipping cost estimation
- **Preferred shipping method** — sea, air, or express
