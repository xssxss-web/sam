MikeOK is a Yiwu-based B2B sourcing agent and wholesale supplier. We help importers, distributors, retailers, Amazon sellers, and wholesale buyers source industrial and hardware products directly from verified Chinese factories.

## Who We Serve

Our clients range from small online sellers placing trial orders to established importers running full-container shipments. We work with buyers from the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa, Europe, North America, and South America.

## Where We Operate

Our office is inside **Yiwu International Trade City**, the world's largest wholesale market with over 75,000 suppliers across 5 districts. Being physically present here means:

- We can visit factories and inspect goods the same day
- We know which suppliers are reliable and which to avoid
- We have direct access to the latest products, pricing, and trends
- We can consolidate orders from multiple suppliers into one shipment

## Our Values

- **Transparency** — No hidden fees, no markup tricks. We charge a clear service fee and pass factory prices to you.
- **Quality First** — Every shipment is inspected before departure. Photos, videos, and reports provided.
- **Long-Term Partnership** — We grow with our clients. Your repeat business is our best reward.
- **Problem Solving** — Supply chain issues happen. We solve them before they reach you.

## How We Work

1. **You tell us what you need** — product specifications, target price, quantity, destination
2. **We source and negotiate** — find verified suppliers, get samples, negotiate best prices
3. **We inspect and consolidate** — quality check, combine orders from multiple suppliers
4. **We ship** — sea freight, air freight, express courier, or FBA direct
5. **You receive and sell** — your goods arrive, ready for your customers
